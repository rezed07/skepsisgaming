Skepsis Season Event

Introduction

Skepsis Gaming Season is a series of tournaments to qualify for the final tournament of $250+ (Total prize pool of all tournaments about $400+). Everyone can register but it is mandatory to be inside our discord server.

General Information

  • The season will last approximately 4 months.
  • All tournaments will be held on the Matcherino website.
  • There are 3 ways to qualify: Points tournaments, Direct qualification, and Last Chance Qualifier (LCQ).
  • All tournaments will have a maximum number of teams and a check-in before starting.
  • All support will be given via our Discord server, in the ticket section.
  • Official Skepsis casters will cast all tournaments.
  • Weekend tournaments have 256 team max, while tournaments on weekdays have a 128 team max.

General Rules

  • Teams that do not have the friendship of the streamer will not be entitled to the prize pool or points.
  • Teams not present inside the server will not receive points.
  • Only Skepsis casters can stream the LCQ and final phases.
  • Age limitations may change depending on Supercell and Matcherino TOS.
  • Account sharing is strictly forbidden.
  • Roster changes are not allowed during the season.
